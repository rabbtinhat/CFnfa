FTX pay back

Crypto February 17, 2025

FTX will start repaying creditors on February 18, 2025, beginning with those in the "Convenience Class," who have claims of $50,000 or less. These creditors will receive full repayment plus 9% annual interest accrued since November 2022. BitGo and Kraken are handling the distributions, which should arrive within 1 to 3 business days after the initial payout date. FTX's collapse in November 2022 was marked by allegations of mismanagement and fraud. Under new leadership, FTX has successfully recovered assets and developed a reorganization plan to repay creditors, with a total distribution of up to $17 billion planned
