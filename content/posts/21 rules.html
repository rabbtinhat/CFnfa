Michael Saylor’s 21 Rules of Bitcoin

Crypto February 18, 2025

Michael Saylor has published his “21 Rules of Bitcoin,” These rules, published on February 18, 2025, aim to provide insights into Bitcoin's potential and reignite investor confidence:

  1. People in the know will buy Bitcoin.
  2. Those who don't understand criticize Bitcoin.
  3. Everyone is against Bitcoin before they are for it.
  4. You will never be able to finish learning about Bitcoin.
  5. Bitcoin thrives on chaos.
  6. Bitcoin is the only game we can all win.
  7. Bitcoin is the only thing in the universe that you can actually own.
  8. Everyone gets Bitcoin at a fair price.
  9. Only buy Bitcoin with money you can't afford to lose.
  10. Tickets to exit the Matrix are priced in Bitcoin.
  11. Knowledge of Bitcoin is limited to those who need to know.
  12. All your models will be destroyed.
  13. The economic cure is the orange pill (BTC).
  14. Be pro-Bitcoin, not anti-fiat.
  15. Bitcoin is for everyone.
  16. Learn to think in Bitcoin.
  17. You don't change Bitcoin, it changes you.
  18. Laser eyes protect you from endless lies.
  19. Respect Bitcoin, or it will make a fool of you.
  20. You will not sell your Bitcoins.
  21. Spread Bitcoin with love.
