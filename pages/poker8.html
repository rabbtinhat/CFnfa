Instructions for Using This Spreadsheet

Tournaments Tab: Record all tournaments played. Enter buy-in, fees, rebuys separately. Profit/Loss and ROI are calculated automatically.

Backers & Action Tab: Track all action sold to backers. Manage markup rates, payments, and calculate individual backer performance.

Expenses Tab: Track all poker-related expenses by category. Flag tax-deductible items for easy reference during tax season.

Reports Tab: View performance summaries, ROI by month, and backer-specific reports. See packages offered and their status.

Tips for Maintaining:

  • Update the spreadsheet promptly after each tournament
  • Keep digital copies of all receipts linked to expense entries
  • Send monthly reports to backers with tournament results
  • Review performance quarterly to identify profitable tournament types/venues
  • Export data regularly for backup and tax preparation

Notes for Distribution to Backers

When sharing reports with backers, consider the following:

  1. Regular Updates: Send monthly statements showing their investment performance
  2. Transparency: Include all relevant tournament details, including fees and rebuys
  3. Payment Schedule: Clearly communicate when payouts will be processed
  4. Tax Documentation: Provide necessary tax forms at year-end for significant winnings
  5. Future Opportunities: Include information about upcoming action packages available
