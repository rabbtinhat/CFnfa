2025 Asset Allocation Outlook

Global Economic Landscape

The economic environment in 2025 is projected to be dynamic, characterized by:

US Fiscal Challenges: Potential government spending cuts and persistent inflation underscore the importance of a diversified equity portfolio and robust inflation hedging tools.

Geopolitical Risks: Ongoing geopolitical tensions, including the Russia-Ukraine conflict, Middle East instability, and trade disputes involving the US, China, Mexico, and Canada, may induce market volatility, prompting investors to seek safe-haven assets like gold.

AI Advancement: The continued advancement of artificial intelligence is expected to drive substantial growth beyond the tech sector, increasing electricity demand and creating investment opportunities in energy (particularly nuclear and natural gas), infrastructure, and utilities.

Asset Class Commentary

Equities

We maintain a positive outlook on equities overall, anticipating them to outperform cash and bonds, although increased market volatility is expected in 2025. We are particularly optimistic about US equities due to their robust economy and dominance in key investment themes like AI.

Fixed Income

Fixed income assets can provide meaningful downside protection due to strong US private sector fundamentals and attractive yields. We recommend increasing allocations to short-term USD fixed income to take advantage of higher yields.

Alternative Investments

We are optimistic about gold and natural resources, with various factors supporting prices and growth potential. Digital assets are poised to reach new all-time highs in 2025 as use cases expand and adoption increases.
