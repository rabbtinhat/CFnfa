Crypto Market Metrics

1. Daily BTC Whale vs. Retail Net Buying/Selling Volume

Source: BGeometrics

Tracks the net daily buying/selling volume of Bitcoin by whales and retail investors. Whale activity is often seen as a market indicator due to their capital influence on prices, while retail behavior reflects broader market sentiment.

2. BTC MVRV Chart

Source: BGeometrics

MVRV compares Bitcoin's market cap to its realized cap (calculated based on the last moved price of each BTC), assessing whether prices deviate from "fair value."

How to Use

  • Overbought/Oversold: MVRV > 3.5 suggests overvaluation; MVRV < 1 implies undervaluation.
  • Trend Reversals: A drop from extreme highs signals bull-to-bear shifts; a rebound from lows may indicate a bottom.

Pros

  • Effective for gauging market profitability via on-chain data.

Cons

  • Fails to predict black swan events (e.g., regulations); historical ranges may shift.
